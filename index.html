🔒 Privacy Policy

Data Collection

SALON-TRACK collects only the information necessary to operate — your name, shop details, customer data, and visit history. This data is stored securely in the cloud.

Data Usage

Your data is used solely to provide the SALON-TRACK service. We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to any third party.

Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Access is protected by your personal PIN. Keep your PIN confidential.

WhatsApp Messages

Reminder messages via WhatsApp are initiated by you. SALON-TRACK does not send messages automatically.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all data at any time by contacting support.

Payments

Payments are processed securely through Razorpay. SALON-TRACK does not store any card information.

Contact

For privacy concerns: support@salontrack.in
