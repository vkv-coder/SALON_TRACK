🔒 Privacy Policy

Data Collection

SALON-TRACK collects only the information necessary to operate the application — including your name, shop details, customer data, and visit history. This data is stored securely in the cloud.

Data Usage

Your data is used solely to provide the SALON-TRACK service. We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to any third party under any circumstances.

Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Access is protected by your personal PIN. You are solely responsible for keeping your PIN confidential.

WhatsApp Messages

Reminder messages sent via WhatsApp are initiated by you. SALON-TRACK does not automatically send messages without your action.

Data Deletion

You may request complete deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting our support team.

Subscription & Payments

Payments are processed securely through Razorpay. SALON-TRACK does not store any payment card information.

Contact

For privacy concerns, please write to our support email available in the Support section.
